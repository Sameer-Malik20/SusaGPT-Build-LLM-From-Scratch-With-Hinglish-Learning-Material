# LLM Engineering and Research Mastery

This folder is dedicated to the deep study of Large Language Models (LLMs). It transitions from foundational NLP to advanced Transformer architectures, covering training, fine-tuning, RAG, and production-grade LLM operations.

## 🧠 What You Will Learn

- **Mathematics for LLMs**: Probability, linear algebra, and calculus specific to language models.
- **Transformers From Scratch**: Building and understanding the core architecture of modern AI.
- **Tokenization & Embeddings**: How machines understand and represent human language.
- **Training Pipeliens**: Pre-training at scale and data curation strategies.
- **Fine-Tuning & Alignment**: RLHF, DPO, and adapting models for specific domains.
- **Inference Optimization**: Techniques like Quantization, LoRA, and speculative decoding.
- **RAG & Retrieval**: Building robust Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems.
- **Long Context & SLMs**: Small Language Models and handling massive context windows.
- **Agentic Workflows**: Integrating LLMs into autonomous multi-agent systems.

## 💼 Career Roles

After completing this folder, you can prepare for:
- **LLM Engineer**
- **NLP Research Scientist**
- **Generative AI Engineer**
- **AI Software Engineer**
- **LLM Solutions Architect**
